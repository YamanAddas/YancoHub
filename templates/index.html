CatByte requires OpenClaw

CatByte is your AI gaming companion, powered by the OpenClaw server. To enable it:

  1. Download or clone OpenClaw
  2. Start the OpenClaw server on port 18789
  3. Restart YancoHub — CatByte will connect automatically

Once running, press Ctrl+B to chat with CatByte about your games.
